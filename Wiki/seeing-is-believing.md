# Seeing Is Believing

> Stub created from [[Sources/Seeing Is Believing (Brain Benders) -- Dr_ Gareth Moore -- Lerner Publishing Group, Minneapolis, 2015 -- Lerner Publishing Group, Inc_ -- 9781467763455 -- 370599a59412dd4879adf1ace20fe996 -- Anna’s Archive]]. Expand manually.

This book, 'Seeing is Believing' by Dr. Gareth Moore, explores optical illusions through interactive puzzles and visual tricks. It categorizes illusions into themes like misleading measurements, color perception, and context-based distortions, offering explanations for why these phenomena occur. The book aims to engage readers in understanding how the brain processes visual information and challenges assumptions about reality through hands-on experiments.
