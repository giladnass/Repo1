This document is a compilation of three books addressing adult ADHD, focusing on concentration, organization, and executive functioning. It provides practical strategies for managing ADHD symptoms, including mindfulness, habit-building, and decluttering techniques. The content emphasizes self-compassion, neurodivergence as a strength, and evidence-based tools for improving focus, reducing overthinking, and enhancing daily productivity. Each book combines scientific insights with actionable exercises tailored for adults with ADHD.
