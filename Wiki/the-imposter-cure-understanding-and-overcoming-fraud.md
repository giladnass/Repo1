# The Imposter Cure Understanding And Overcoming Fraud

> Stub created from [[Sources/The imposter cure _ how to stop feeling like a fraud and -- Dr_ Jessamy Hibberd -- 2019]]. Expand manually.

This book introduces imposter syndrome as a widespread experience affecting people across all demographics, characterised by an inability to internalise successes and a persistent fear of being exposed as a fraud. The author, a clinical psychologist, explains that sufferers attribute achievements to luck while owning all their mistakes, creating a self-reinforcing cycle of doubt. The book aims to help readers understand the psychology behind these feelings, challenge the faulty beliefs that sustain them, and develop practical strategies for building genuine self-confidence. It is structured in three parts: understanding imposter syndrome, reframing the belief that you are an imposter, and learning techniques to overcome it permanently.
