# ADHD Clinical Reference Guide

> Stub created from [[Sources/ADHD-APG_Full_Booklet_WEB-DL_Secured_2021-11-09]]. Expand manually.

This document provides healthcare professionals with comprehensive, up-to-date information on ADHD, covering epidemiology, pathophysiology, diagnostic and clinical trial scales, and biostatistical methods. It highlights increasing ADHD prevalence across age groups, common comorbidities, neurobiological mechanisms involving brain structure/function and neurotransmitters, and tools for assessing symptoms and treatment outcomes. The guide emphasizes practical applications of scales for diagnosis and research, alongside statistical interpretation of clinical data.
